Frequently Asked Questions

General

Do you offer warranty?

A minimun 5 year warranty is offered with every purchase with an additional charge to upgrade to an extended warranty. Customer satisfaction is very important to us, all waranties cover any damage done provided proof of so.

How do I install it?

Installation is a tricky process. However, our team has made tutorials to help you get through it. Furthermore, if you still find yourself stuck reach out to us for further assistance.

How is your company helping the environment?

We use 100% recycled plastic in the manufacturing of the plastic casing around our electronics. Also, we try as much as we can to use recycled electronics from old screens. However, since these are much less accessible than recycled plastic we sometimes have to resort to creating our screen from non-recycled electronic parts.

How much does it cost?

The cost for the basic screen (without the camera) is $99. For the premium version that includes both the screen and the outside view camera, the cost is $149.

Products

How does the screen work?

Every screen is mounted to the window with a camera between the screen and the window. You are able to display any picture you want or choose from the library provided in the picture library. The camera is used to display the live images of the view outside for people who aren't afraid of seeing what's outside.

What materials are your prdoucts made of?

The frame is made up of Aluminum 1100 with carbon fibre fittings.

Is the display good?

The display is a very important feature of our product, for that reason we have partnered with Samsung to provde exceptional quality. Our screens are 4k HDR and range in sizes per requested by the customer.

Do you provide software updates?

As well as just a product you are purchasing a service. Our devices are hooked up to the internet and connected to our server. Our software department will release updates to improve the functionality and efficiency of the product.

Shipping

What countries do you ship to?

We offer worldwide shipping!

How fast will I receive the product?

We offer 1-2 business day express shipping for North-American customers, but we also offer shipping internationally that takes 5-7 business days.

How much does shipping cost?

Express shipping is an extra $12.00 CAD while standard national shipping is free. We also offer international shipping which is an extra $5.00 CAD.

My package has not arrived yet, what can I do?

If you are experiencing a delay in delivery contact our support and we will handle everything from there! Please have your confirmation email and credentials of payment ready.

Contact

What are your hours of operation?

Our team is active business hours and will respond to emails 7 days a week.

Where can we reach you?

Our business emails and social media accounts are located at the bottom of the page. You can send an email or a message through those platforms.

Do you have a phone service?

As of right now we do not have contact via phone. However, our team is very dedicated to our customers and will respond in 1-2 buisness days through email.
